Mojulo Gallery



    


    

        Input an equation and hit run to see the results!
        
 If the result is a black square, it's evaluating to low numbers only (0-10)
    

    

        Available inputs:
        
 x, y
        
 time (measured in frames, starts at 1)
        
 r, A (Polar Co-ordinates)
        
 +,-,*,/,^,%
        
 ()
        
 sin(), cos(), tan()
        
 sqrt(), rand(), abs()
        
 0xffffff
        
 bitwise operators: |, &, ^|, >>, <<
